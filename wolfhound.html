About Irish Wolf Hound

  • Potential Genetic Diseases: Elbow dysplasia; Gastric dilatation volvulus; Hip dysplasia; Eye Disease:  Progressive retinal atrophy (PRA), Multifocal retinal dysplasia (MRD), etc.
  • Other Reported Diseases: Cancer: Osteosarcoma; Dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM); Atrial fibrillation (AF); Rhinitis; Pneumonia; Wobblers syndrome – breed clubs report as uncommon; Osteochondrosis – shoulder and stifle; Portosystemic shunt, etc.
  • Irish Wolf Hound is the tallest of all dog breeds, and is one of the breeds with the shortest life spans. It was built for stamina and endurance, which means that it needs enough space to do plenty of exercises every day.
  • Average Life Span: 6.5 years
