About French Bulldog

  • Potential Genetic Health Issues: The breed has a short nose, which can cause: Brachycephalic obstructive airway syndrome (BOAS), restrict oxygen intake, difficulties with panting causing overheating, cleft lip and palates; Cherry eye; Hip dysplasia; Hereditary cataract (early onset) (HC);
  • Eye testing in brachycephalic dogs should be routine as a foreshortened head/face can cause substantial welfare problems throughout a dog’s life.
  • French Bulldog usually needs a nose surgery to solve the health issues caused by its nose. It is a complicated but necessary surgery for the dog’s basic needs.
  • Average Life Span: 11 years
